Arrival in Negombo - 2 Nights & 3 Days

Negombo is a city on the west coast of Sri Lanka, north of the capital, Colombo. Near the waterfront, the remains of the 17th-century Dutch Fort now house a prison. Negombo Lagoon, lined with fishermen’s huts, feeds into the Dutch-era Hamilton Canal. The canal leads south to Colombo. Neoclassical St. Mary’s Church, completed in the 1920s, features a ceiling decorated with vivid religious paintings. Weather: 30°C, Wind W at 16 km/h, 76% Humidity Once all guests are arrived, We shall introduce all of the team to each other and do a briefieing about the Sight-seeing planned for the next 2 days

Heading Towards Hikkaduwa - 1 Nights & 2 Days

Early Morning We shall do Whale Watching Trip & Head towards Hikkaduwa, Hikkaduwa is a seaside resort town in southwestern Sri Lanka. It’s known for its strong surf and beaches, including palm-dotted Hikkaduwa Beach, lined with restaurants and bars. The shallow waters opposite Hikkaduwa Beach shelter the Hikkaduwa National Park, which is a coral sanctuary and home to marine turtles and exotic fish. Inland, Gangarama Maha Vihara is a Buddhist temple decorated with hand-painted murals.
