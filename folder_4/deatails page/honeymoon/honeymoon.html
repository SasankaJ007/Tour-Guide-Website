Bandaranaike International Airport to Negombo

After arriving at Bandaranike International Airport, you all are welcomed by the team and then you will be transfered to Ranweli Holiday Village at Negombo.

Negombo to Kandy

After having breakfast at the hotel, you can enjoy a nice time at the beach & lagoon. Next you will travel to Kandy. En-route will visit to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage at Pinnawala, a sanctuary and breeding ground for elephants where you can enjoy watching the adults and babies bathe in Maha Oya River. After having lunch, you will be taken to the hotel AMAYA HUNAS FALLS in Kandy and cool yourselves in the pool and rest. Later, in evening you will be visiting the Temple Of The Sacred Tooth Relic which is a Buddhist temple in the city of Kandy which houses the relic of the tooth of the Buddha. After visiting the temple, you will arrive back at the hotel for overnight stay and dinner.

Kandy

Enjoy a romantic Breakfast in Bed with a complimentary fruit basket & resort-made chocolates. After breakfast at the hotel, enjoy a city tour to discover some populer tourist spots in the Kandy. You can visit Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya which includes more than 4000 species of plants where you can have a walk through the flower gardens. After lunch, if you are willing to you can visit Ramboda Tea Factory. Later, after arriving back to hotel you can engage in one complimentary activity from Boat ride, Mini golf or Cycling which is provided by Hotel AMAYA HUNAS FALLS. After engaging in an activity you will have a special candlelit dinner at a location of your choice.
